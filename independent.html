Machine Learning Independent Study 2023/2024

What it is

This is my 2023/2024 Independent Study! It's essentially a self-guided study of Machine Learning that I designed on my own. It is partitioned into several units, each covering a different part of Machine Learning. This independent study is more of a preliminary one where I touch upon each section use them to make programs that can be applied to the real world. I intend to do another one next year where I go in depth in a particular domain of ML or a specific project that I find to be interesting

How & Why I made it

As aforementioned, I created the entireity of the study's curriculum. Once I created this curriculum, I presented it to my Computer Science teacher who approved it, allowing me to allocate a period during the school day to pursue it. I created the study becuase I believed that it would be an amazing oppurtunity for me to learn about a domain that isn't only quite prominent in todays world, but is one that I find very interesting

Curriculum

  1. Unit 1: Supervised Learning
  2. Unit 2: Unsupervised Learning & Model Evaluation
  3. Unit 3: Neural Networks
  4. Unit 4: Text Proccessing
  5. Unit 5: Experimental programs

Program Integration

Whenever I learn a new topic from my Independent Study, I often either create programs that apply it, or I integrate it into an existing program. Such is the case with programs like the prioritized to-do list, Theta Terminal, and more in the future

Machine Learning Independent Study 2023/2024

What it is

This is my 2023/2024 Independent Study on Machine Learning. It's partitioned into units covering different aspects of Machine Learning, where I create practical programs for real-world use. Next year, I aim to focus on a specific area of Machine Learning or an interesting project.

How & Why I made it

As previously mentioned, I developed the entire curriculum for this study. I presented it to my Computer Science teacher, who approved it, enabling me to dedicate a period for it during the school day. I created this study because I believed it would provide me with an excellent opportunity to explore a domain that is not only prominent in today's world but also personally compelling.

Curriculum

  1. Unit 1: Supervised Learning
  2. Unit 2: Unsupervised Learning & Model Evaluation
  3. Unit 3: Neural Networks
  4. Unit 4: Text Proccessing
  5. Unit 5: Experimental programs

Program Integration

Whenever I learn a new topic through the study, I either create programs that apply it, or I integrate it into an existing program. Such is the case with programs like the prioritized to-do list, Theta Terminal, Intellivest, and more in the future
